Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We do not collect any personal information from our users.

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Introduction

At Makitox, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to user data and privacy when you use our mobile applications and services.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. All data processing happens locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and data ownership.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users.

This means we do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Names or personal identifiers
  • Device information or unique identifiers
  • Usage data or app interaction patterns
  • Sleep pattern data or health metrics
  • Audio data or recordings
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Any other personally identifiable information

All app functionality operates entirely on your device. Your data stays with you, always.

How Our Apps Work

Since we don't collect any user information, our applications operate entirely on your device:

  • Local Processing: All features and functionality work directly on your device
  • No Accounts Required: No registration, login, or personal information needed
  • Offline Capability: Apps function without internet connection
  • Data Ownership: You own and control all your data
  • Privacy by Design: Built from the ground up with privacy as the core principle

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data.

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no user data to sell, trade, rent, or share with third parties. Your information stays on your device at all times.

Data Security

Your data is completely secure because it never leaves your device.

Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no security risks related to data transmission, storage breaches, or unauthorized access to our servers. All your data remains on your device under your complete control.

Data Retention

We don't retain any data because we don't collect any data.

All app data (such as sleep patterns, preferences, and settings) is stored locally on your device. You have complete control over this data and can delete it at any time through your device's app settings.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data because it stays on your device.

Since we don't collect any personal information, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) don't apply to our services. However, you have something better: complete ownership and control of all your data on your device.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@makitox.com

Children's Privacy

Our applications are safe for users of all ages because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no privacy concerns related to children's use of our applications. All functionality operates locally on the device.

International Transfers

There are no international transfers of your data because we don't collect any data. All information stays on your device regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Continued use of our applications after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@makitox.com

We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns you may have and will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.
